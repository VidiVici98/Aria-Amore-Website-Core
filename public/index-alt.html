Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about booking with Aria Amore

How far in advance should I book? ‚ñº

We recommend booking 3-6 months in advance for weddings and major events, especially during peak season (May-October). However, we can often accommodate shorter notice for smaller events based on availability.

What types of events do you perform at? ‚ñº

We perform at weddings, corporate galas, holiday parties, private celebrations, anniversaries, proposals, and more. Any occasion that deserves exceptional musical entertainment is perfect for live opera.

Can I request specific songs? ‚ñº

Absolutely! We have an extensive repertoire and love to accommodate special requests. During our consultation, we'll discuss your favorite pieces and suggest options that fit your event's mood and theme.

Do you provide sound equipment? ‚ñº

Yes! All our packages include professional sound equipment and setup. For larger venues or outdoor events, we can arrange enhanced audio systems to ensure perfect sound quality for all your guests.

What's your cancellation policy? ‚ñº

Cancellations made 90+ days before the event receive a full refund minus a 10% processing fee. 30-90 days: 50% refund. Less than 30 days: no refund, but we'll work with you to reschedule when possible.

Do you travel for events? ‚ñº

Yes! We're based in Charleston, SC, but regularly travel throughout the Southeast. Travel fees may apply for distances over 100 miles. Contact us to discuss your specific location.
