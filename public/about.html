Frequently Asked Questions

Is there a travel fee for performances outside your local area? ➕

The first 30 miles of travel are included at no cost. Beyond that, a travel fee of $1 per mile applies. For performances over 100 miles away, overnight accommodations must be provided by the client.

How do I secure my booking? ➕

A non-refundable deposit of 50% of the total fee is required to reserve your event date and performers. The remaining balance is due no later than 14 days before the event. Bookings made within 14 days of the event must be paid in full at the time of booking.

What is your refund and cancellation policy? ➕

Cancellations made more than 30 days before the event are eligible for a refund of payments made beyond the non-refundable deposit. Cancellations within 30 days are not eligible for a refund. In cases of unforeseen circumstances, partial refunds or rescheduling options may be considered at the company’s discretion.

Can I reschedule my event? ➕

Yes, rescheduling requests are allowed with at least 21 days’ notice and depend on performer availability. If a new date cannot be arranged, the booking will be treated as a cancellation under our refund policy.

What happens if the event is affected by weather or unforeseen events? ➕

If an event must be postponed or canceled due to unavoidable circumstances such as natural disasters or government restrictions, payments may be applied toward a future booking within six months. Refunds are not issued in these cases, but rescheduling is supported.

What are the requirements for the performance setup? ➕

Clients must provide a safe and appropriate performance space with suitable acoustics, lighting, and sound equipment if needed. A detailed program and any special requests should be finalized no later than 14 days before the event.

What if my payment is late? ➕

Payments not received by the due date may result in cancellation of the booking with no refund of the deposit. To avoid disruption, please ensure all balances are paid by the deadlines stated in your agreement.

Can I make changes to my booking after it’s confirmed? ➕

Yes, changes such as time adjustments or repertoire updates may be requested up to 14 days before the event. Major changes may incur additional fees depending on scheduling and preparation needs.

How can I book Aria Amore for my wedding or event? ➕

Contact us today to begin planning your dream performance! We’ll guide you through selecting repertoire, confirming details, and securing your date with a deposit.
