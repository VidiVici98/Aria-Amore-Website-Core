Frequently Asked Questions

Can I request specific songs? ➕

Absolutely! Our performers love accommodating personal requests. We just ask for at least two weeks notice so we can prepare your chosen pieces.

Do you perform outdoors? ➕

Yes. Outdoor performances are welcome when shade and a safe setup are provided. We bring our own amplification and sound system if needed.

How far in advance should I book? ➕

We recommend booking at least three months ahead for weddings and one month for private events to secure your desired date.

What if I need to cancel? ➕

We understand plans can change. Cancellations made more than 14 days prior receive a full refund; after that, partial refunds may apply.
